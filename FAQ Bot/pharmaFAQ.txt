Q: What medicines do you offer?
A: We offer a wide range of prescription and over-the-counter medicines including antibiotics, pain relievers, vitamins, allergy medications, and chronic disease management drugs. Our inventory includes medicines for fever, cold, allergies, diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and many more. We also stock specialty medications, health supplements, and wellness products.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply search for your medicine using the search bar, add it to cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to upload a valid prescription for prescription medicines. After adding your delivery address and payment details, confirm your order. You'll receive order confirmation via SMS and email immediately.

Q: What is your delivery time?
A: We deliver medicines within 24-48 hours in metro cities and 2-5 days in other areas. Same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan locations for orders placed before 12 PM. Once dispatched, you'll receive tracking information to monitor delivery in real-time. Our delivery partners work 7 days a week to ensure timely delivery.

Q: Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?
A: Yes, we deliver 7 days a week including weekends and most holidays. Our commitment is to ensure you never run out of essential medicines. Order tracking is available in real-time through our website and mobile app. During major holidays, delivery times might be slightly longer.

Q: How much is the delivery charge?
A: Delivery is free for orders above Rs. 500. For orders below Rs. 500, a nominal delivery charge of Rs. 50 applies. Bulk orders or subscription orders may have special delivery arrangements. We also offer free express delivery for premium members. Check delivery charges at checkout before confirming your order.

Q: Can I return medicines?
A: Unopened and undamaged medicines can be returned within 7 days of delivery for a full refund. The medicines must be in their original packaging with seals intact. Used or opened medicines cannot be returned due to safety regulations. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number and reason for return. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Q: Do you require a prescription?
A: Yes, for prescription medicines (antibiotics, steroids, controlled substances, etc.), we require a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner. This is a legal requirement to ensure patient safety. Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines like pain relievers, vitamins, and wellness products can be purchased without prescription. Our pharmacists verify all prescriptions before processing orders.

Q: How do I upload my prescription?
A: During checkout, you'll find an option to upload your prescription. You can click a photo using your phone camera, upload from gallery, or use our app's built-in camera scanner for better quality. Accepted formats include JPG, PNG, and PDF. Make sure the prescription is clear, readable, and includes doctor's signature and registration number.

All online transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. You can save your payment methods for faster checkout in future orders.
